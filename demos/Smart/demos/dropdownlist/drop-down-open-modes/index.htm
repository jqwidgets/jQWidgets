Modes Description

Auto - the dropDownList is opened/closed when the element is hovered.
Default - the dropDownList is opened/closed when the element is clicked.
DropDownButton - the dropDownList is devided in two components. An Action Button and a dropDown Button. The dropDownList can be opened/closed when the dropDownButton is clicked.
None - the dropDownList can't be opened/closed.
